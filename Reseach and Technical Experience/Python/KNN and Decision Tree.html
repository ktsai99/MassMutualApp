Question 10

Purity of leaves in terms of entropy are on a scale of [0,1]. We can observe that at the root it is classifying class L where the majority go into the second node. We have 3228 training samples in total. Most of the samples 2746 samples branch on to class 2 on the right side where the left over,482, go to the left side. We can observe the difference in entropy as they go descend to max depth classifying year, V, and D. Out of all the leaves, only one leaf is pure as shown by entropy 0 in class 1. This leaf is pure because all the 81 samples belong to this class 1. The rest of leaves are all inpure because they have a split between class 0 and class 1 as shown by their entropies > 0. Unpure leaves mean that further classification may be needed determine where a sample is in either class 0 or class 1 in order to generate a leaf closer to pure. In this class, all the leaves but 1 are pure so the tree should require a higher max depth.
